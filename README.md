# Nigeria Economic Indicators Analysis 2015-2024

## Overview
An analysis of Nigeria's key economic indicators from 2015 to 2024, 
including inflation, exchange rate, GDP growth, oil prices, and foreign 
reserves.

## Key Findings
1. When the Naira weakens, the price of importation becomes more expensive, 
resulting in higher inflation values. A correlation of 0.94 reflected this relationship.
2. The GDP growth of Nigeria is heavily tied to the price of oil, expressed 
in a positive correlation of 0.78 between oil prices and national GDP growth.

## Data Source
Data sourced from Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), National Bureau of 
Statistics (NBS), and World Bank published records.
