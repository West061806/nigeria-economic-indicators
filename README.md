# Nigeria Economic Indicators


##Objective: To analyse Nigeria's key economic indicators from 2015 to 2024 using Python, including inflation, exchange rate, GDP growth, oil prices, and foreign reserves.

##Findings

1. When the Naira weakens, the price of importation becomes more expensive, resulting in higher inflation values. A correlation of 0.94 aptly reflected this

2. The GDP growth of Nigeria is heavily tied to the price of oil, this was expressed in the positive correlation of 0.78 between oil prices and national GDP growth

##Data Source: Data sourced from Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), National Bureau of Statistics (NBS), and World Bank published records.

##Tools: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
